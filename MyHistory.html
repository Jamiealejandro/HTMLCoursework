Venezuela

My father works in the oil industry and has done for the past 30 years. Due to this, i was born and spent the first couple years of my life in the now torn country of Venezuela. I grew up speaking spanish, as i was sent to a venezuelan nursery during the day but eventually had to leave the country once my parents found a new job elsewhere.
